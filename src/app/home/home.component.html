A unified Ag platform for data sharing and licensor/producer relations.

Kaprella's mission is to be the go-to platform for collaboration and improving business relationships between licensors and producers.

Technology has had a profound impact on agriculture throughout human history. Irrigation techniques, motorized farm equipment, plant genetics, and GPS are examples of advancements that have allowed farmers to cultivate and manage increasingly larger amounts of land.

Kaprella is designed to be the collaborative platform for licensors, breeders, and producers in a complex multi-national industry.
